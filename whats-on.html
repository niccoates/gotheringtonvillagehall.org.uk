What's On

Regular Events

Always something on

Monthly  ·  First Saturday, 10:30–12:00

Village Coffee Morning

Drop in for freshly brewed coffee, home-made cakes, and a bacon butty. A wonderful chance to meet neighbours old and new. Free entry — all are welcome.

Regular hire  ·  Various times

Fitness & Exercise Classes

The hall provides a great space for yoga, pilates, aerobics, and other fitness activities. If you run a class and are looking for a local venue, get in touch.

As required  ·  By arrangement

Private Parties & Celebrations

Birthdays, anniversaries, christenings — the hall makes a great venue for private celebrations. Fully self-catering, with good facilities and a large car park.

Weekly  ·  Thursdays

Squirrels & Cubs

Gotherington Squirrels and Cubs meet weekly at the hall on Thursdays. Part of the local Scout group, open to children in the village and surrounding area.

Weekly  ·  Fridays

Beavers & Scouts

Gotherington Beavers and Scouts meet weekly at the hall on Fridays. A great opportunity for young people to learn new skills, make friends, and explore the outdoors.
