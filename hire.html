The Space

What's available

The main hall is a bright, spacious room suitable for everything from birthday parties and community meetings to fitness classes, performances, and private functions.

The hall is fully wheelchair accessible and benefits from on-site parking, a fully equipped kitchen, and WiFi throughout.

Booking

How to book

Booking is straightforward — you can either check availability and make an enquiry online through our HallMaster booking diary, or get in touch with us directly by email.

Once your booking is confirmed, you'll receive details of the hall's terms and conditions of hire. We ask all hirers to familiarise themselves with these before their event.

Ideas

What people hire us for

Parties & Celebrations

Birthday parties, anniversaries, christenings, retirement dos — the hall is a great self-catering venue for any celebration.

Fitness & Classes

Yoga, pilates, dance, martial arts — the hall's open floor and accessible facilities make it ideal for regular fitness bookings.

Meetings & Events

Community groups, club nights, AGMs, presentations — we provide a neutral, well-connected space in the heart of the village.
