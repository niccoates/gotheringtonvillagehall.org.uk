Welcome

A village hub since 1905

Gotherington Village Hall has served the community for over 120 years. Originally established as a Reading Room in 1905, it has grown into a warm and welcoming space for social, recreational, and educational gatherings of all kinds.

Situated on Malleson Road in the heart of Gotherington, Gloucestershire, the hall is run by a dedicated team of volunteer trustees and is available to hire for private parties, club meetings, fitness classes, and much more.

Whether you're a long-standing resident or new to the village, the hall is here for you.
